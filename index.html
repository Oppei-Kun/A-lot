My thoughts and emotions

Dear me,
You're a jumbled mess
Fake personas serve as copium
A broken ego__
A fragile heart
Though a bright mind
Horrible yet bearing
You face challenges you make
Thorw your life into your burning passion
Just to rise up as a Pheonix
You're the wind__
Your powerful thoughts the debris
Ode to me as I stare into this mirror.
